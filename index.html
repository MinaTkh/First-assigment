Let's have a pizza night!

Treat yourself and your loved ones to some homemade pizza.

  • How to do it
  • Topping suggestions

Base ingredients. 4 portions.

Dough

- 25g yeast
- 2,5dl water
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Â½ tsp salt
- 7dl wheat flour

Sauce

- 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 pinch of salt
- 1 clove of garlic
- A pinch of basil

How to do it

1. Mix the yeast and the water.
2. Add oil, salt and most of the flour.
3. Work up a dough and let it sit for 30 min.
4. Mix the tomatoes with chopped garlic, oil, salt and basil.
5. Bring up to a boil and let it simmer for 20 min.
6. Spread out the dough into a circle of about 1.5cm thickness.
7. Cover the dough with the tomato sauce.
8. Add the topping of your choice.
9. Bake in the oven at 200C for about 15 minutes.

Topping suggestions

- Salami and cheese
- Ruccola and walnuts
- Ham and pineapple

Buon appetito!
